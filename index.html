Important facts about radiators and our services

Proudly Lebanese Quality

Nehme Radiators manufactures high quality radiators that exceed market standards at competitive prices, proudly made in Lebanon.

Complete Car Service

We can service your car from A to Z. There are times you just need us and not the mechanic for all your radiator and cooling system needs.

Radiator Function

A radiator's primary role is to cool down the engine coolant, preventing overheating and ensuring optimal engine performance.

Coolant Replacement

The coolant/anti-freeze must be replaced every 30,000 km or every year to maintain optimal cooling system performance.

Professional Maintenance

Your cooling system must be checked yearly by a professional technician to ensure durability and prevent costly repairs.
